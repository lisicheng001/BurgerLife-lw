üé™ How to Play

Playing BurgerLife is as easy as flipping a burger! Here's how to get started:

1. Take Orders: Listen carefully to what each customer wants. They'll tell you exactly how they like their burger prepared.

2. Cook the Patty: Place the burger patty on the grill and cook it to perfection. Don't let it burn!

3. Add Toppings: Stack lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, pickles, and sauces according to the customer's order.

4. Serve Fast: Deliver the completed burger quickly to earn maximum points and tips!

5. Upgrade & Expand: Use your earnings to buy new ingredients, upgrade your equipment, and make your restaurant even better!
